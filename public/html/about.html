Meet our Founders

DJ Prado

DJ is Co-owner and Co-founder of Doggie Spaw. DJ oversees our facility to ensure that we provide the highest standard of care. She works closely with the staff and customers to ensure quality communication and customer satisfaction. Prior to opening Doggie Spaw, DJ studied Veterinary Sciences in college and was a veterinary technician for 3 years. DJ also has a decade of investment experience in the financial service industry. She is quick to tell you that co-founding Doggie Spaw is the culmination of her interest in small business and her passion for pet care.

Amanda Lowell

Amanda is Co-owner and Co-founder of Doggie Spaw. In addition to managing our facility & staff, Amanda is also your “go to” person for any questions or concerns. She performs client consultations and evaluates new dogs to make sure they are a good fit for our unique environment. Prior to co-founding Doggie Spaw, Amanda was the general manager of a cage-free dog daycare & boarding business in Dallas. There, she oversaw all areas of operations including staffing, payroll, and facility maintenance to ensure that all dogs enjoyed a safe and healthy experience.

Ryley Chapman

Ryley is Co-founder, Groomer (extraordinaire), and Salon Director at Doggie Spaw. Ryley’s compassionate handling skills foster a stress-free experience for a happy, healthy and stylish groom. Ryley is known for his technical expertise, artistry and the strong connections he develops with both his clients and their dogs. Prior to joining us, Ryley was Director of Student Training at Fisher’s Academy for Professional Pet Groomers. His emphasis on safety, handling, and client communications instilled confidence in his students and the dogs under their care.

Our Mobile Spaw

Every inch of Doggie Spaw was thoughtfully designed to provide your dog with the best possible experience. Our modern mobile grooming salon is designed to reduce stress and support your dog’s well-being with elevating tubs & tables, top-of-the-line grooming equipment and high-quality natural bathing products. We also know that our services are only as good as our people. Our facility is staffed with dog-loving pack leaders and experienced groomers who are trained in dog behavior, CPR & first aid, and dedicated to bringing out the best in your dog. When using Doggie Spaw services, you will always have peace of mind knowing that your dog is safe, comfortable and happy!
